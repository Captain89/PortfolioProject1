About Us

Hi Welcome to DoggyWoggy! we are an independant dog-walking, meet and greet website for anyone who is passionate about taking their little (or not so little!) best furry friends on a fun and friendly walk with others.
We strive to meet all your expectations and needs whilst on our walks, and we currently have three different walks available which we hold weekly. Events and times / bookings, can be made below in our 'Join in' section. We are able to meet our walkers and their dogs at a local 'Meet Me' station, where there will be some refreshments provided, including Tea, Coffee, Healthy snacks and Healthy Dog treats and water too!
