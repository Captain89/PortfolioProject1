About Us

Hi Welcome to DoggyWoggy! We are an independant dog-walking, meet and greet website for anyone who is passionate about taking their little (or not so little!) best furry friends on a fun and friendly walk with others.
We strive to meet all your expectations and needs whilst on our walks, and we currently have three different walks available which we hold weekly. Events and times can be found in our Join in section below, these are updated weekly if there are any changes our guests need to be aware of. Bookings/ Registrations can be made below in our Contact section. We are able to meet our walkers and their dogs at a local 'Meet Me' station on each walk where there will be some refreshments provided including Tea, Coffee, Healthy Snacks and Healthy Doggy Treats and Fresh Water, before we start and after we finish! (Return to the same station upon finishing a walk). Scroll down for further information and even book a Walk with us!
